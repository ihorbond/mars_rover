Welcome to Mars Rovers remote control console.
Commands:
'F' to move rover forward
'B' to move rover in reverse
'L' to rotate rover left (counter clockwise)
'R' to rotate rover right (clockwise)
'R' and 'L' commands rotate the rover into a desired direction (N, W, E, S).
Multiple commands accepted up to 20.
Labels:
G - grid
@ - rover Kata
$ - rover Napoleon
# - obstacle
